Our Pricing Philosophy

At Mrs. Garland’s Bakery, pricing is based on real ingredient costs, packaging, preparation time, and small-batch production. We aim to keep our prices affordable while still supporting a sustainable home bakery.

How Prices Are Calculated

  • Ingredients: Flour, sugar, butter, eggs, dairy, fruits, nuts, chocolate, and specialty items.
  • Packaging: Disposable loaf pans, single-serve containers, cupcake liners, boxes, and bags.
  • Preparation Time: Mixing, baking, cooling, assembling, and packaging.
  • Batch Size: Small batches baked fresh each weekend.

Why Some Flavors Cost More

Certain flavors require additional ingredients or preparation steps. For example:

  • Chocolate and specialty extracts add expense.
  • Pistachio items use premium nuts and are priced accordingly.

Why Prices May Change

Ingredient and packaging costs fluctuate throughout the year. When supplier prices increase, our menu prices may be adjusted to continue providing high-quality baked goods.

Our goal is always to remain fair, transparent, and affordable.
